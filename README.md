# CX_Vanguard_Project 

## Evaluating the Impact of a Digital Experience Redesign

## Project Overview

This project analyzes the impact of a redesigned digital customer journey implemented by Vanguard as part of an A/B experiment.

The objective was to determine whether the new digital experience improved customer outcomes by increasing completion rates, reducing friction throughout the process, and enhancing overall process efficiency.

Using Python for data preparation and Tableau for visualization, we explored customer behavior across the digital journey and translated analytical findings into business recommendations.

---

## Business Problem

Vanguard introduced a redesigned online experience for a group of customers (Test Group) while another group continued using the existing interface (Control Group).

The main business question was:

> **Does the redesigned customer journey improve the client experience and increase successful process completion?**

To answer this question, we analyzed customer interactions, process efficiency, completion rates, and behavioral patterns across different customer segments.

---

## Project Objectives

* Clean and prepare customer interaction data.
* Build business KPIs to evaluate experiment performance.
* Compare Test and Control groups.
* Identify customer segments that benefited the most from the redesign.
* Create interactive Tableau dashboards.
* Communicate findings through data storytelling and business recommendations.

# Key Insights

* The redesigned experience increased overall process completion.
* Customers completed the process more efficiently.
* The largest improvements were observed among customers over 70 years old.
* The Test group also recorded a higher number of errors, suggesting the need for further investigation before a full rollout.
* Overall results indicate that simplifying the customer journey positively impacts customer success.

---

# Business Recommendations

* Expand the redesigned customer experience to a larger user population.
* Investigate the origin of the increased error rate.
* Continue monitoring customer behaviour after deployment.
* Conduct qualitative usability research to complement quantitative findings.
* Prioritize accessibility improvements for older customer segments.
